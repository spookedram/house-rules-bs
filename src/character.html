Details

Appearance

They are short, wide, and built with a large torso and small feet. They have long red hair tied back with gold ribbon into a traditional bun. They're wearing monk robes, wraps on their hands and feet, no shoes, and a pack over their shoulder. They have dark skin, bright yellow eyes, and a prominent nose.

Personality

They are excited by action and rush into battle without fear, but deep down they fear dying young and hope to live long enough to settle down and have a family and a peaceful life.

Backstory

Abandoned as a child, they grew up in a monastery, learning philosophy and self-defense from the kind monks who lived there. The monks became their family, but soon they felt a need to seek out their birthplace and set out on a quest to find their parents.
